Privacy Policy

This privacy policy is applicable to the FlashBack app (hereinafter referred to as "Application") for mobile devices, which was developed by FlashBack Devs (hereinafter referred to as "Service Provider") as a Free service. This service is provided "AS IS".

This privacy policy is effective as of 2026-03-18.

DATA STORAGE

User-generated content, such as study materials and progress data, is primarily stored locally on your device.

If you choose to use online features (such as browsing, downloading, or publishing community lists) and sign in with your Google account, your data (including saved lists, community-shared lists, and study progress) will be synchronized and stored on secure cloud servers via Google Firebase. This ensures backup and community functionality. Private lists created while signed out remain local and are not transmitted to the Service Provider.

WHAT INFORMATION DOES THE APPLICATION COLLECT?

The Application itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information directly. The Application offers an optional account system via Google Sign-In for community features and cloud synchronization. All core study features remain accessible offline without an account.

When you sign in with Google, the following information is collected and stored:

  • Your Google display name (used to generate an initial username, which you can change)
  • Your Google user ID (used for authentication and data association)
  • Your profile photo URL (displayed in the settings screen)

No email addresses, passwords, or other sensitive personal data are collected or stored by the Service Provider.

The Application uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you or your device:

  • Google Play Services (https://policies.google.com/privacy)
  • Google Firebase (https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy): Used for cloud data storage (Firestore), user authentication (Firebase Authentication), and analytics (Firebase Analytics).
  • Google AdMob (https://policies.google.com/privacy): Used for displaying advertisements.
  • Google Sign-In (https://policies.google.com/privacy): Used for optional account authentication.
  • Klipy GIF Service (https://klipy.com/support/privacy-policy): Used for displaying GIF content within flashcards, which may include sponsored content.

COMMUNITY FEATURES AND USER-GENERATED CONTENT

The Application allows signed-in users to share study lists with the community. When you share a list, the following information becomes publicly visible to all users:

  • Your username (which you can change at any time)
  • The list name, description, and content (terms and definitions)
  • Download and usage statistics for the shared list

You can remove a shared list at any time by toggling off the sharing feature, which will delete the list and its content from the cloud servers. Other users who have downloaded your list to their local device will retain their local copy.

IN-APP PURCHASES AND PREMIUM FEATURES

The Application may offer optional premium features or subscriptions in future updates. All payment processing will be handled securely by Google Play In-App Billing. The Service Provider does not collect, store, or have access to your credit card or billing information.

INTERNET USAGE

The Application is designed to work primarily offline. An internet connection is required for certain optional features including multimedia content (GIFs via Klipy), community list browsing and sharing, Google Sign-In, and the operation of third-party services like Firebase and AdMob. All core study features function without an internet connection.

DOES THE APPLICATION COLLECT LOCATION INFORMATION?

No. The Application does not collect or access any precise location data from your device. Note that third-party advertising SDKs (such as AdMob) may collect approximate location or device information for ad targeting purposes; you can manage these preferences through your device settings.

DOES THE APPLICATION USE ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE?

No. The Application does not use any artificial intelligence or machine learning technologies.

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

The Application does not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information through the Application, please contact the Service Provider at flashbackapp.official@gmail.com.

DATA RETENTION

Offline Data: For users who do not use the Google Sign-In feature, all data is stored strictly on the device. Uninstalling the Application will permanently delete all locally stored data, and the Service Provider will have no way to recover it.

Cloud Data: For users who sign in with a Google account, study lists, community-shared lists, and progress data are stored on secure Firebase servers. This data is retained as long as your account is active to provide backup and community functionality.

Third-Party Data: Data collected by third-party services (such as Firebase Analytics or AdMob) is subject to their respective retention policies.

ACCOUNT DELETION

You can delete your account at any time through the Application's Settings screen. Deleting your account will permanently remove:

  • Your user profile and username
  • All lists you have shared with the community (including their content)
  • Your study progress data stored in the cloud

Locally stored data on your device will not be affected by account deletion. You may also request account or data deletion by contacting us at flashbackapp.official@gmail.com.

YOUR RIGHTS AND OPT-OUT

You can stop all data usage by the Application by uninstalling it using the standard uninstall process on your device. You can delete your cloud data by deleting your account through the Settings screen. For data collected by third-party services (Ads/Analytics), you may opt-out through the device settings (e.g., resetting advertising ID) or by following the instructions provided by those services.

DATA SECURITY

Offline data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers. Synced data is transmitted via encrypted connections to Google Firebase servers. The Service Provider takes all reasonable measures to ensure the integrity and security of the Application and its data.
